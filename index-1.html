About Pahiram

Pahiram.ph was created for a good mission for the upliftment of every Filipino citizen who has difficulty moving due to lack of financial needs. This is a type of poverty alleviation and contribution to the country's economy. The goal of Pahiram.ph is to provide immediate help or solutions to those in financial need such as stressed students, employees, and small business owners who need additional business investment. A quick process for a quick solution for their financial problem.

We are recognized for transparency and fairness in the way we deal with our stakeholders. Our goal is to help every Filipinos optimize their full potential and achieve major growth. We provide affordable credit to the underserved guided by best-in-class management systems and leading practices. We lead a motivated, competent and professional workforce.
